Pure Toolshed

Apps the team has built to take the friction out of the working day. Find one, open it, or build your own and add it to the wall.

Add your tool

  1. Open index.html in the repo and click the pencil to edit it in the browser.
  2. Copy one of the blocks in the apps list near the top of the file.
  3. Fill in the name, a one-line description, the category, your name, the link to your app, and whether it is public or private.
  4. Commit the change. The wall updates itself.

Before you add it

If a tool stores or sends candidate or client personal data, keep it in a private repo and get sign-off before listing it here. Tools that work entirely in the browser, with nothing leaving the page, are fine to share openly.
